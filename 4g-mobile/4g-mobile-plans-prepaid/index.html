Terms & Conditions and other Important Information’s:

  • Top Ups are available from Rs 10 to Rs 1000 with denominations in multiples of 10 and with amount other than
    above plan denominations. For example, 10, 20 50, 100, 300 or 950. Such top ups will have balance of recharge
  • amount, minus 18% GST and 7% Service Charge, for ex 10rs top up will have 7.5 Rs balance.
  • Plans 199 to 999 have daily data cap of 5 Gb. 
  • Data is for 4G, if customer has 3G handset, it will get 10 percent of pack data. For example, in Simple
    299 pack, it will get 3 Gb of data.
  • Up to 6 consecutive recharges can be queued in My Exicom app, excluding FRC and SRC.
  • FRC is only applicable to new customers, port in customers and customers on Exicom Welcome Offer. SRC
    is available only on approval and offer basis.
  • Present customer can continue with their old plans for another 6 months for 5 April 2017. After which
    they have to choose a new pack/plan.
